About me

I was born in Moscow, where I studied educational psychology and language teaching. Assisting people on their path to discovery is still one of my favourite things.

My journey to UX design started with creating surveys at the schools I worked with. I was eager to help confused people make sense of digital products, where I began to understand true powers of human-centred design and accessibility.

Working with learners of all ages has taught me that feedback is gold. It is the ability to listen, observe and ask the right questions that moves the design process forward.
